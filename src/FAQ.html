About Formula One Tracker

Welcome to our F-1 Tracker! We're proud to share our first team week project at Epicodus. This tracker will allow the user to get live results for:

  • Drivers standings.
  • Constructors standings.
  • The schedule for the current season.

Our tracker utilizes Ergast.com for the API. Our website wouldn't scale well with this resource, as it is has limited free API calls. However it was a great fit for our project.
